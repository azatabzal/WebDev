Beshbarmak

  • Time: 2 hour
  • Serves: 20 minutes
Ingredients lamb or beef with bone large onion peeled and sliced into rings divided bay leaf eggs plain flour
Quantity 2 1/2 lbs 1 1 2 about 600 grams

Step 1: Put your meat and 1/2 the onion in a deep enough dish to cover it with cold water leaving enough space on the top so that it doesn't boil over.

Step 2: Bring to the boil, constantly removing the foam, Reduce heat to simmer when it starts to boil. Cover it.

Step 3: Let it cook for about 2 1/2 hours or until the meat is tender and falling off the bone.

Step 4: Meanwhile, prepare your noodle dough.

Steak

  • Time: 2 hour
  • Serves: 20 minutes
Ingredients lamb or beef with bone large onion peeled and sliced into rings divided bay leaf eggs plain flour
Quantity 2 1/2 lbs 1 1 2 about 600 grams

Step 1: Pat dry – use paper towels to pat the steaks dry to get a perfect sear and reduce oil splatter.

Step 2: Season generously – just before cooking steaks, sprinkle both sides liberally with salt and pepper.

Step 3: Preheat the pan on medium and brush with oil. Using just 1/2 Tbsp oil reduces splatter.

Step 4: Sear steaks – add steaks and sear each side 3-4 minutes until a brown crust has formed then use tongs to turn steaks on their sides and sear edges (1 min per edge).
